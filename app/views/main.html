About Stock Eleven

We provide a stock screening dashboard for building your investment portfolios

Our rules are simple: buy cheap stocks with high value, but do it when the market push them.

For doing this, we provide lists of more promising stocks and to prove it, we have build our portfolios and monitoring them on daily base. We have build Stock Eleven for us and made it available for everyone.
